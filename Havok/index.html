Everywhere gamers play

Our goal is to empower developers to deliver immersive experiences wherever their players play. Havok products are supported and optimized across all major platforms, including Nintendo Switch, PlayStation®, and Xbox. We provide integrations for Unity and Unreal Engine and are used in countless proprietary game engines.

Havok powers the next generation

Developers can now realize their visions with more fidelity than ever before. Havok is setting a new standard for next-gen experiences with games like Gears Tactics on Xbox Series X|S and Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart, optimized for PlayStation 5®.

Havok Cloth™

Customizable, fully multithreaded, and highly optimized, Havok Cloth enables creators to add physically based motion to garments, hair, banners, foliage and other soft bodies.
